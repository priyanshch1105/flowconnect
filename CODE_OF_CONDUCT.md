# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate with patience.
- Offer feedback about code and ideas, not people.
- Help keep discussions inclusive and useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that would make collaboration unsafe or exclusionary

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, please contact the maintainers privately through the security or repository contact channel once those details are finalized.
